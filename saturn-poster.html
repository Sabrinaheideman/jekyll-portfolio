put a ring on it

This is a concept of a poster ad if ever one day it is possible to travel to Saturn. I kept a blue theme for the colour of Saturn and 1/8th of its moons because the different shades bonded the piece together creating a strong poster that is easy on the eyes. The title placement is to create a structure balance between the illustration and the title. After creating this poster, I got inspired to make illustrations of all the planets in our Solar System.
