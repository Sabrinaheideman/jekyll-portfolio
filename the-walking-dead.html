the walking dead logo animation

You can probably tell by now that I’m obsessed with zombie related things. So of course, I had to make a logo animation of The Walking Dead. The logo is used like a window to see into the world of the walking dead. The city rises up and then the zombies. I used a red theme because the show can be very gruesome at parts. I also added a grunge to make the logo look worn.

Disclaimer: I did this on my own time, independently from the show and I do not own any of the audio for this video.
